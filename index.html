On April 5, 2022, we launched a free website for a welfare organization as part of our commitment to giving back to the community. We recognized the importance of providing a platform for the organization to reach a wider audience, share their mission and impact, and engage with supporters.

An Educational Website

SIKI-EDUCATION

On January 2, 2023, we launched a website aimed at providing people with the opportunity to learn freelancing skills and courses. We recognized the growing demand for online learning and the need for a platform that could help individuals develop the skills they need to succeed as freelancers. Our website provides a range of courses and learning resources across various industries, including graphic design, writing, web development, and more.

Who we are

We are Siki, a company committed to making a positive impact in the world. Our two websites, one dedicated to education and the other to welfare, reflect our belief that knowledge and social responsibility go hand in hand, and that everyone deserves access to resources that can help them thrive.

"You don't have to be great to start, but you have to start to be great."

As a good company, there are several things that we need to prioritize to ensure long-term success and sustainability. First and foremost, we need a clear and compelling vision and mission that inspires our team and guides our decision-making. We also need a talented and motivated team of employees who are committed to delivering high-quality products and services, and who feel valued and supported in their work

with a focus on social responsibility and environmental stewardship, and maintain a solid financial foundation to ensure long-term growth and profitability.
